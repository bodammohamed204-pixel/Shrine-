# Shrine Store Listing

## App Name

Shrine

## Short Description

Create, search, and manage respectful memorial profiles with private account access.

## Full Description

Shrine helps you create and browse memorial profiles in a simple mobile experience. Add a memorial with a name, dates, country, photo, and personal notes, then search your saved entries whenever you need them.

The app includes private account setup, WhatsApp OTP activation, profile information, country browsing, followed profiles, blocked users, contact support, and terms screens. Your entries are designed around respectful memorial management and easy discovery.

Key features:
- Create memorial profiles with custom details.
- Search by name, country, or information.
- Browse memorials by country.
- Follow profiles from your account.
- Manage profile details and app language.
- Block entries you do not want to see.
- Activate accounts with WhatsApp OTP.
